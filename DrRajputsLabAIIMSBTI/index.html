Meet the Team

A passionate team of scientists, clinicians, and students united by the mission to decode stem cell therapy & cancer biology and translate discoveries into patient benefit.

Principal Investigator

Dr. Sanjay Kumar

· Additional Professor
Department of Biochemistry, AIIMS Bathinda

Dr. Sanjay Kumar leads the Stem Cell Therapy & Cancer Biology Laboratory, focusing on molecular mechanisms of cancer progression, PDE5 inhibitor-based therapeutics, and liquid biopsy diagnostics. His translational research bridges fundamental molecular biology with clinical oncology to identify novel cancer biomarkers and therapeutic targets.
